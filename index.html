About The Job

Key features of our company

History

I would presume that most people wouldn't even know what a Futurist even is or even what they do, but they have an extreme impact on the Earth we live on and they deserve more acknowledgement. Normaqlly known as Futurists or Futurologists are social scientists who specilize in futurology and attempt to explore predictions about the future and how they can emerge from the present, whether that of human society in particular or of life on Earth in general.

Passion

There is a reason why I specifically chose Futurist as the job for this project. I have always wanted to be one after knowing what they actually do and how they do it. The job sounds incredibly complex and hard but to me, its incredibly interesting because how much they impact the advancement of technology.

Design

One very important part of being a Futurist is being able to code in multiple programming languages and to be able to design pieces of technology from different units such as biology which you could make a solar powered Water Filter or in Astronomy where you could be able to design a satellite. Futurists design technology for multiple technological companies for their use to prevent events from harming their company or anyone associated with them. Plus, futurists also work the government to design weapons and predict moves in war.

Support

Futurists support multiple companies with the development of future technology and bringing it to the present as fast as possible. One of the most popular creations made by Modern Futurists is foldable oled screens. As most people know, Samsung is creating a phone in the future that can fold in half turning the device from a size of a standard mobile phone into a 7" screen instantly. Futurists create this technology to help people building businesses and finding plans to stop global threats such as Global Warming. Futurists make a very big impact on our lives, and we don't give them enough credit.
